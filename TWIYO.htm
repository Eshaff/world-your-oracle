Which sense awakens your insight?

Are you visual, auditory, or kinesthetic?

  • Visual
 If you tend to be visually oriented, Oracle will help you develop your “second sight” – with techniques like scrying, dream incubation, or balloon diagrams.
  • Auditory
 If hearing affects you most, Oracle contains techniques for listening to the “whisperings of intuition” – like chanting, listening to birdsong, or asking an ancestor for advice.
  • Kinesthetic
 If touch nurtures you or you’re always in motion, Oracle will connect you with your “body wisdom” or “gut feelings” – through journaling, using a mudra, or sculpting clay.
