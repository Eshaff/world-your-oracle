The World is Your Oracle is a compilation of multicultural divination techniques from around the world – both ancient and contemporary – designed for each individual’s unique situation, desire, and intuitive style. It gathers together dozens of methods, one-third of them visual, one-third auditory, and one-third kinesthetic. You can select a techniques based on what appeals to you in the moment, a technique that dovetails with your intuitive strength (visual, auditory, or kinesthetic), or simply work your way through all the techqnieus for fun and greater self-knowledge.

ENERGIZE your INTUITION like the great ARTISTS, SCIENTISTS, and ENTREPENEURS of yesterday and today

Today we’re living in an environment of rapid change, where analytical thinking no longer suffices. DISCOVER your INNATE ABILITY to KNOW

Unlike other types of divination, which offer generic feedback, the oracles in this book show you how to channel our own abilities and tune into the messages surrounding you for specific guidance to your most pressing issues. It’s your receptivity to the truths buried in your unconscious that unlocks these divinations. You don’t have to believe in angels and spirit guides to use them.

Instead The World is Your Oracle encourages spiritual development by connecting you to your deepest wisdom. Once you begin to put oracular insights into practice you’re on the path to authentic personal growth.

LEARN which SENSE AWAKENS your INSIGHT

Are you visual, auditory, or kinesthetic?

Visual If you tend to be visually oriented, Oracle will help you develop your “second sight” – with techniques like scrying, dream incubation, or balloon diagrams.
Auditory If hearing affects you most, Oracle contains techniques for listening to the “whisperings of intuition” – like chanting, listening to birdsong, or asking an ancestor for advice.
Kinesthetic If touch nurtures you or you’re always in motion, Oracle will connect you with your “body wisdom” or “gut feelings” – through journaling, using a mudra, or sculpting clay.
